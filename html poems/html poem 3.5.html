VII

Frog  faces,  frog  lust,  frog  bellies in  the  dust,

                                                                                                                                                                               till  unexpected  flame gave  you  another  name:

(  there ’s   the   siren   wail   again,   May   15  ;

                                                                                                                                                                              by  the  clock ,  near  6 ,  that’s  4  by  the  sun  ):
